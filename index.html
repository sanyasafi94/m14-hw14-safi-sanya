About Me!

Sanya Safi is a Pakistani American based in Chicago, holding an undergraduate degree in Applied Psychology from the University of Illinois at Chicago and currently pursuing a master's in web design. Professionally, she contributes her skills to You Matter Inc., a Chicago-based non-profit organization focused on disseminating resources and information related to youth mental health. Demonstrating an entrepreneurial spirit, Sanya recently established a crochet small business, Loop to Loop. Her personal interests include the art of crochet, enjoying audiobooks, and caring for a local cat colony.

Sanya is someone who is always ready to learn and absorb knowlege. When she becomes interested in a topic, there is no stopping her from learning as much as she can about it.

Crochet has become something of a lifestyle for Sanya. She enkoys it prefusely and has even started a small business in hopes of creating something with this activity that she loves so much.

The cat colony in Sanya's backyard have become family more than random cats that she takes care of. She makes sure that they are well fed and taken care of.
